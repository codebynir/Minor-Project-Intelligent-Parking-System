About Us

Intelligent_Parking_System

The "Intelligent Parking System" is here to reduce the drivers effort and time to search parking space. We are providing details of vacant parking slots of registered organisation. Organisations can register themselves by signup as a member to get our service. Intelligent Parking System uses parking lots cameras to manage the number of cars that can be parked in given space. We are providing all the information about parking lots to the user so that user can get the parking lots in an easier way.
